Florals

We stock 1000's of flower stems of the highest quality, in both botanically correct and designer colors and styles. We custom design your arrangement in the style and size you require. When ordering a custom floral arrangement, keep in mind: Finished Dimensions (height, width and depth). Desired Overall Shape (rounded, famming, tall, short, one sided or all around). Style (archetectural, garden, traditional, contemporary, parellel, bidermeyer or layered)
